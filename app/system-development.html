Case study

Let's have a look at Eloquia. This private school internal system made staff management, accountancy, student communication and their progress assesment much easier. The system provides different levels of access and rights for each user type.

Aviation Language Assessment Tool, enabling smooth language proficiency level assessment, was one of the components of the platform.

Our role

To design the product's architecture we analyzed the internal company processes, their needs, main groups of users, their problems and the goals each part wanted to achieve.

We have implemented the solution from scratch, tested it, and developed additional functionalities needed.

The result for the company was no more repetitive work, faster access to information, easier progress assessment, and absolute clarity of what's going on in the company.

Technology and approach

The base of the good cooperation in this kind of system is comprehensive specification that puts both the client and developers on the same page before we start working on the sytem. In order to keep the assessment tool safe and secure, we implemented the logic that prevented users from abusing the application. We achieved this by effective communication between the browser and the central server of the platform.

We've used Domain Driven Design approach and with close cooperation with the client defined the domain details and designed the architecture. This helped especially when we needed to develop the booking system, which was planned to be flexible and configurable.
